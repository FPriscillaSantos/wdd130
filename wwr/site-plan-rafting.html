Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to bring joy to the people who come to raft in our company and enjoy leisure time in complete safety and in contact with nature. Our programs include monitors and children's activities so no family or group of friends will be excluded.

Audience

Who are the target customers? I want to focus on families, as there are many rafting options, but families with young children are always excluded from such programs. Our purpose will be to prepare packages with monitors carrying out activities close to the rafting site where children unsuitable for rafting can have other leisure opportunities What do they want? A website that shows all leisure options and perks. How will my audience usually access my website (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Cell phone
